# Exynos Developer Society

## Management and Resources

- **Managed by**: Samsung S. LSI.
- **Resources**: Offers a range of tools and training resources for development on the Exynos platform.

## Best Lab Platform

- **Function**: A platform where administrators select projects demonstrating excellence and potential.
- **Project Submission**: Individuals cannot directly submit projects; administrators curate and showcase projects.

## Account Security

- **Lockout Policy**: An account gets locked after five consecutive incorrect password attempts.
- **Unlocking Process**: Locked accounts can be unlocked through a verification process via steps sent to the linked email address.

## Support and Assistance

- **Public Forums**: Users are encouraged to participate for support with development tools.
- **Personalized Assistance**: Can be obtained by submitting queries through the "Contact Us" page.
