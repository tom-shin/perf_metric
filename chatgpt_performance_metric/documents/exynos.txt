# Exynos Chipsets

## Exynos 2200 Chipset

### CPU and GPU

- **CPU Configuration**: Cortex®-X2, Cortex®-A710, and Cortex®-A510.
- **GPU**: Samsung Xclipse 920.

### AI Capabilities

- **AI Engine**: Dual-core NPU and DSP.

### Camera Capabilities

- **Single Camera Support**: Up to 200MP.
- **Dual Camera Setup**: 64MP + 32MP at 30fps.

### Video Performance

- **Decoding**: Up to 8K at 60fps.
- **Encoding**: 8K at 30fps.
- **Supported Formats**: 10-bit HEVC (H.265) and VP9.

### Display Capabilities

- **4K/WQUXGA**: Up to 120Hz.
- **QHD+**: Up to 144Hz.

### Gaming Features

- **Technologies**: Hardware-accelerated ray tracing and variable rate shading.

### Supported Devices

- **Smartphones**: Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, S22+, and S22.

### Manufacturing and Integration

- **Process**: 4-nanometer EUV.
- **CPU Cores**: Armv9.
- **Modem**: 5G, compliant with 3GPP Release 16.

### Security and Additional Features

- **Security**: Integrated Secure Element, robust encryption.
- **ISP**: Supports up to 200MP, AI-integrated functionalities.
- **Fourier Transform**: No native support; third-party libraries recommended.

## Exynos 2400 Chipset

### CPU and GPU

- **CPU Configuration**: Cortex®-X4, Cortex®-A720, and Cortex®-A520.
- **GPU**: Samsung Xclipse 940.

### AI Capabilities

- **AI Engine**: 17K MAC NPU (2-GNPU+2-SNPU) and DSP.

### Camera Capabilities

- **Single Camera Support**: Up to 320MP.
- **Dual Camera Setup**: 64MP + 32MP at 30fps.

### Video Performance

- **Decoding**: Up to 8K at 60fps.
- **Encoding**: 8K at 30fps.
- **Supported Formats**: 10-bit HEVC (H.265) and VP9.

### Display Capabilities

- **4K/WQUXGA**: Up to 120Hz.
- **QHD+**: Up to 144Hz.

### Gaming Features

- **Technologies**: Console-quality graphics and advanced GPU technology.

### Supported Devices

- **Smartphones**: Samsung Galaxy S24+ and Galaxy S24.

### Performance and Connectivity

- **AI Performance**: 14.7x improvement over predecessor.
- **Modem**: 5G NR, supports up to 9.6Gbps downlink in FR1 and 12.1Gbps in FR2, compliant with 3GPP Rel.17.
- **Connectivity Enhancements**: Supports NTN satellite connections, integrated GNSS block.
